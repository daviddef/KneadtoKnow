## Consistency notes (paste into every prompt)

- **Character:** the same friendly Pixar-style Italian pizza chef — white chef's
  hat, big brown moustache, rosy cheeks, white shirt with rolled sleeves, blue
  denim apron, red neckerchief. Warm, smiley, gentle. Use the character
  reference sheet every time so he stays on-model.
- **Setting:** cosy rustic pizzeria kitchen — wood-fired brick oven glowing in the
  background, colourful tiled walls, jars of ingredients on shelves, wooden
  bench dusted with flour, warm golden lighting. Colourful "PIZZA" letters on the
  wall.
- **Camera:** medium shot, chef centre/slightly facing camera, eye-level, shallow
  depth of field. Keep him in frame the whole time.
- **Length & loop:** 6–10 seconds. **Start and end on a similar pose** so it loops
  seamlessly (the app loops these silently until tapped).
- **Mood:** playful, bouncy, wholesome — like a kids' cooking show. Gentle motion,
  no fast cuts.
- **Audio:** include a short cheerful line of dialogue (and light kitchen/foley
  if the tool allows). Keep it clean and clear. The app starts muted; kids tap to
  hear it.
- **Format:** 1280×720 (16:9), MP4 / H.264. I'll downscale + compress for the app.

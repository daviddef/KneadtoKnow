Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Kneady Pizza is designed to work entirely on your device. We don't have accounts, we don't show ads, and we don't use analytics or third-party tracking.

What we store

Your settings, favourite recipe and in-progress bake are saved only on your device. They are never uploaded to us.

Location

If you choose to fetch your kitchen temperature automatically, the app asks for your location and sends your approximate coordinates once to the free Open-Meteo weather service to read the current temperature. We don't store your location, and it isn't linked to you or used to track you. This is entirely optional — you can set the temperature by hand instead.

Notifications

If you turn on reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device for upcoming dough steps. These never leave your device.

Data we collect

None that is linked to your identity. The only data that leaves your device is the coordinate described above, used solely to provide the temperature feature.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information.

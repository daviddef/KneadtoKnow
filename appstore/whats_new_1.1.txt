Introducing Kid Mode — a big, fun, mess-and-giggles way to make pizza with little ones!

• Pick a pizza (Hawaiian, Margherita, Dino Pepperoni and more) or build your own and save it.
• Choose your dough: "Right now!" (ready in ~30 mins) or "Puffy & bouncy".
• One giant step at a time, in kid words — with cups and spoons (and grams for grown-ups).
• Confetti, jokes and happy cheers all the way, plus a grown-up hand-off for the hot oven.

Also in this update:
• Reminders now only ping you once you've actually started baking.
• Tap any cooking step to read it full-screen, then swipe between steps.
• The pizza picker stays put while a bake is underway, so you don't lose your place.

Buon appetito! 🍕
